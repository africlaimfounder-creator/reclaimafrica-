Simple Process

From search to settlement
in three steps.

01

Search Your Name

Enter your name, BVN, or NIN. We scan all 12 asset types simultaneously across registrars, insurers, banks, and government databases across Africa.

02

Verify Your Identity

Complete our secure KYC process once. We generate all required documents and communicate with every institution on your behalf.

Receive Your Money

Recovered funds land directly in your bank account. We charge a small success fee only when you get paid. Zero cost if we find nothing.

Reclaim Africa — Founder Intro

The Founder
Emmanuel Godwin · Founder · Delta State 🇳🇬

Building Africa's
financial future
from the ground up.

Emmanuel Godwin is a 25-year-old first-time founder, author, and content creator from Delta State, Nigeria. He is building Reclaim Africa entirely bootstrapped — operating solely from an Android phone with no technical co-founder and no external funding.

His mission is simple but powerful: return money that already belongs to ordinary Africans. The ₦215 billion sitting unclaimed in Nigeria's capital market is not a government problem — it is a technology problem. And Emmanuel is building the solution.

He is also the author of The Blind Civilization — a book exploring how Africa's challenges were shaped by colonial systems, aimed at educated young Africans and the diaspora.
