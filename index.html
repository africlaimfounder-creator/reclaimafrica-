Simple Process

From search to settlement in three steps.

No mountains of paperwork. No confusing portals. Just a clean guided journey from search to your bank account.

1

Search Your Name

Enter your name, BVN, or NIN. We scan all 12 asset types simultaneously across registrars, insurers, banks, and government databases across Africa.

2

Verify Your Identity

Complete our secure KYC process once. We generate all required documents and communicate with every institution on your behalf.

3

Receive Your Money

Recovered funds land directly in your bank account. We charge a 10% success fee only when you get paid. Zero cost if we find nothing.

The Founder

Emmanuel Godwin

Author · Entrepreneur · Pan-Africanist

I am a 25 year old first-time founder from Delta State, Nigeria. I built Reclaim Africa entirely from an Android phone — no laptop, no funding, no technical co-founder.

The idea came from my book The Blind Civilization — an exploration of how Africans overlook the wealth on their own continent. When I discovered ₦215 billion in unclaimed dividends sitting idle in Nigeria, I realised the book was right.

So I stopped writing about the problem and started building the solution.
