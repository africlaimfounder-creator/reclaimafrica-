# Reclaim Africa 🌍

Africa's first unified platform for recovering unclaimed financial assets.

## What We Do
Reclaim Africa helps individuals and families recover:
- 📈 Unclaimed dividends
- 🏦 Dormant bank accounts
- 🛡️ Insurance payouts
- 👴 Pension funds
- 💼 Unclaimed salaries
- 📱 Mobile money
- ⚖️ Inheritance assets
- 📜 Government bonds
- 🤝 Cooperative funds
- 📊 Investment accounts
- 🧾 Tax refunds
- 🏠 Property proceeds

## The Problem
N215 billion in unclaimed dividends sit idle in Nigeria alone. Millions of Africans are owed money they don't even know about.

## Our Solution
One platform. 12 asset types. Pan-Africa.
